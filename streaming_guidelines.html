Streaming Guidelines

When streaming or recording videos of games from the Nightshade label, the following guidelines are recommended:

  • Provide commentary, reactions, or other transformative aspects combined with the gameplay video. (Try to avoid providing silent, gameplay-only video.)
  • Include a link to the game in the stream/video description, and (optionally) within the video itself, if possible.
  • Full playthroughs and monetization are acceptable, but keep in mind the first note. It is also recommended to be careful about potential spoilers in titles and thumbnails.
  • Check the notes below for details specific to certain games.

Game Notes

  • Eldritch Academy: Safe
  • Eldritch University: Can be made mostly stream-safe by avoiding these specific choices: [“Only tonight, okay?”], [I could use a happy distraction…], [I want Misaki. I love her.]
  • Life as Designed: Unsafe (sexual language in dialogue and narration)
  • Blooming Nightshade: Safe
  • Flowering Nightshade: Unsafe (sexual language in dialogue and narration)

Safe: This game should be safe to stream in its entirety.
Unsafe: This game is not safe to stream, and may require editing to produce a gameplay recording that would be safe.

Some games may have additional information about mature content warnings included on their Steam pages.
