Important Notes

Mizuki tends to be serious, particularly when it comes to her hobby of playing music. She spares little time for people she doesn't think are taking things seriously, and actively calls out those who hurt herself or her friends. Despite this no-nonsense approach, she cares very deeply about the people she trusts and can be counted on to be loyal.
