Important Notes

This character's details are a work in progress.

Hageum often wears her hair in a high ponytail, using a black ribbon hair tie with green stripes. She has her family's green eyes, which are usually rare among the Hye people.

Hageum's family has Sagafodtsk ancestry mixed into their bloodline, which sometimes manifests in certain traits that diverge from what is otherwise expected from Hye individuals. In particular, Hageum has a larger and more robust build and thicker eyebrows than most other Hye women. More noticably, she has inherited her family's uncommon dirty blond hair, which not everyone in her family has (and thus inspired her name, "summer gold").

Hageum is energetic and confident. Her early life was comfortable and safe, leaving her with an optimistic view of the world. She can be easily swept up by her curiosity when it comes to learning new things, especially if it involves foreign cultures. She can also become incredibly focused when reading, studying, or working for her family's inn.
