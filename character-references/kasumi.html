Important Notes

As a teenager, Kasumi tends to wear playful outfits with lots of bright colors and designs. After starting university, she tries to start dressing in a more “mature” way that leans into office casual style. She will occasionally hold her arms in ways that emphasize her breasts, but rarely recognizes that she’s doing so.

Kasumi is friendly and cheerful, but isn’t always good at reading a situation. Having acquired a reputation as being an airhead, as an adult she deliberately tries to be less impulsive and act more mature. After several traumatic experiences involving people abusing her trust, she begins to focus more on maintaining a small group of friends rather than trusting everyone she meets. However, she never fully loses her friendly nature, and she is willing to give new friends a chance as long as they act in good faith.
