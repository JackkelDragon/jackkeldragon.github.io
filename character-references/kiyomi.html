Important Notes

Kiyomi usually wears warmer clothing than others might in most situations, as she gets cold more easily due to a genetic condition.

Kiyomi is generally calm and down-to-earth, but this sometimes makes her feel like she gets overlooked. She tends to express her annoyance with deadpan sarcasm. She can also be fairly lustful, but is good at disguising it enough to go unnoticed.

Story

Kiyomi Ito is technically an orphan, but has little memory of her parents and has been raised by her widowed uncle as if she were his own, having a sister-like relationship to her cousin. She doesn’t stand out much, but she tends to be a loyal friend. She started working as a QA analyst during university, and continues to work there after graduating.

Note: The details of Kiyomi’s life vary across the different world states (storylines) of the games she appears in. Some common traits are:

  • Becoming friends with Himeka, Izumi, and a few other girls during high school at Solana Academy.
  • Moving to Akiramine to attend university and get a job.
  • Struggling to decide what she wants from her life, leading to a period of disillusionment.
