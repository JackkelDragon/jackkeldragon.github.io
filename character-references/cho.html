Important Notes

Cho has heterochromatic eyes, with each having a different color iris. She also has a tuft of hair that doesn’t seem to go the same direction as the rest (cowlick/ahoge).

As a teenager, Cho is sometimes mistaken for a tomboy at first glance because she wears masculine clothes to school, but this is only because she had repurposed the rest of her clothing for cosplay. Once her interest in cosplay is something she doesn’t feel the need to hide, she begins to wear more traditionally feminine clothing.

Cho is mixed race (Japanese mother, Irish father), resulting in her being taller than the other girls she knows as well as giving her a pale complexion and freckles.

Cho is generally fairly casual and laid-back, but has a bashful side when people try to get to know her. While she gets better at opening up to others after high school, she begins to doubt herself and the choices she makes. As a young adult, she feels reliant on others to make up for her mistakes.
