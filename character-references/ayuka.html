Important Notes

Ayuka Sawada lives in a share house in the city of Yamashiya, where she works as an office assistant for a furniture company. She’s not particularly invested in her job, and so she spends much of her downtime in the common areas of the share house, prodding the other residents to tell her about their lives. She always seems to have the hardest time when people move out, despite acknowledging that most people don’t intend to stay at the share house forever. She is quick to bring men home, which sometimes worries her friends and the other share house residents, since she never seems to pick men who intend for a long-term relationship.
