Welcome to the Workshop

Published: Sat 09 March 2019
By Jackkel Dragon

In misc.

tags: updates

Note: This was originally posted as the introduction to the WordPress.com website.

Ever since some of the old sites that I used to post news and information about my projects went down or became ghost towns (such as the slow decline of my Tumblr views followed by the mass exodus from that site), I’ve been considering putting together a site specifically about my projects. It’s still a work in progress, but this WordPress site is the first step into making something like that a reality. There are still a number of things that need tweaking and a lot of my older writings aren’t listed yet, but hopefully this site will help people keep track of the things that I’ve been up to. (And with how interconnected some of the Nightshade universe stories can get, that might become useful in sorting out what characters have been in which stories.)

To start with, the site has a basic list of all of my released original stories so far, from Shireishi to Eldritch Academy. I also started a list of fanworks/mods that I’ve worked on that are still potentially worth looking at, and may add more to that list from my old fanworks. I’ve also made a list of unannounced, unreleased, and tentative ideas for projects to give an idea of what might be coming next. For the first two lists, each entry also has a link to where that story/game/etc. can be found.

As I make more things, I may make further use of this blogging functionality of WordPress to make the kind of long-form updates that I used to do on Tumblr (and are hard to do on Twitter). I also hope to eventually be able to clean things up a bit so that the website doesn’t look so bare-bones, but that may be a longer-term goal.

In the meantime, I hope those who have found their way here manage to find something they like. For instance, maybe the Steam release of Eldritch Academy in about two weeks will be interesting to some people who missed the original itch.io release. Either way, I hope to be able to put out more stories and games as we move forward. I hope you’re looking forward to it.
