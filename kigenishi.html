Kigenishi

These stories take place in Kigenishi「起源石」, a fantasy setting loosely based on Sengoku Japan and East Asian mythology. While the stories primarily focus on the island of Kigenishi itself, some stories may expand beyond the borders of Kigenishi to other lands in the setting.

Setting Information

These articles provide information regarding the Kigenishi setting. In the case of conflicting information, details referenced directly in stories take priority over the setting notes. (However, information only provided in-character may still be inaccurate or incomplete.)
