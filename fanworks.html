Team Despair

A team of two that made Corpse Party fangames, particularly the Corpse Party D2 series.

Writer/Designer: Jackkel Dragon
Character Design/Illustration: Hiragi-HOUX (Hollie Moir)

Corpse Party D2: Depths of Despair

Status: Completed (v10)
Downloads: v10, v9, v8, v7, Demo v6

A Corpse Party fangame based on the D-2 [Black Magic] ending of Corpse Party Rebuilt, a remake of the original Corpse Party by KENIX SOFT that was translated by Memories of Fear.

After the events that cost Ayumi Shinozaki her friends, she became a recluse that lived with her sister. Still haunted by nightmares of that day, she reluctantly attends an informal class reunion, six years after the tragedy. But she soon finds herself in the old schoolhouse once again, this time alone as she faces her nightmares turned into reality…

Translated into Japanese by Kazumi for v10.

Corpse Party D2: Depths of Despair (Nightmare Edition)

Status: Demo (v2) (story complete)
Downloads: Demo v3 (test), Demo v2 (Windows|Mac), Demo v1

The Nightmare Edition is a remake of the original Depths of Despair, with a revised script and a new layout for the old schoolhouse that is closer to the layout of the school in the original Corpse Party. In addition, numerous improvements from Fatal Operation and Zero Hope have been integrated into this version of the game.

Corpse Party D2: Fatal Operation

Status: Demo (v6) (story complete)
Downloads: Demo v7 (test), Demo v6 (Windows|Mac), Demo v5 (Windows|Mac), Demo v4, Demo v3, Demo v2, Demo v1

A sequel/companion story to Depths of Despair, taking place several days after the “Mended Soul” ending of that game.

Six souls with troubled pasts find themselves in a recreation of the old Divine Blessing Hospital, a version of the hospital that was home to tragedy and suffering. They must work together to find a way out alive, but the tensions between survivors threaten to tear them apart…

Corpse Party D2: Zero Hope

Status: Demo (v3) (story complete)
Downloads: Demo v3

An indirect sequel to Depths of Despair and a direct sequel to Corpse Party Zero, taking place a year after the “Mended Soul” ending of Depths of Despair and seven years after the B-rank ending of Corpse Party Zero.

Kaori Hasegawa, now a teacher, still struggles with the loss of her little sister. When she and her students end up in the haunted schoolhouse of Heavenly Host Senior High, she must decide whether her loyalties lie with her students or her sister.

Corpse Party D2: The Dragon Mask

Status: Demo (v1) (story 40% drafted)
Downloads: Demo v1, Demo v0

An indirect sequel to Depths of Despair, taking place about a month after the “Mended Soul” ending of Depths of Despair.

The paranormal troubleshooter Aiko Niwa has seen her fill of tragedy, and has taken on a new job in hopes of averting future strife: Hunt down a cursed mask. But someone or something seems to be trying to keep it from her grasp, and she must rely on a theatre troupe as one of her few leads in finding the mask before it claims more victims.

Note: Work on this game has been indefinately suspended. In the meantime, a story summary with concept notes can be found here.

Game Mods

Helarine, Doomguide of Kelemvor (BGEE/BG2EE NPC Mod)

Status: Beta v1 (BGEE and BG2EE) (90% of BGEE story drafted, 10% of BG2EE story drafted)
Downloads: GitHub Repository

A new priest companion for Baldur’s Gate 1/2 Enhanced Edition. Travel with a woman who claims to know death more intimately than others, who has converted to the rising faith of the new god of the dead–Kelemvor. Discover what happened to her during the Time of Troubles that set her on the path to accepting the place of death, and how she ties into the legacy of that conflict.

Scars of War (Dragon Age: Origins Campaign Mod)

Status: Beta 1.00 (story complete)
Downloads: Nexus Mods Entry

The Fifth Blight has begun, and Ferelden is caught in the midst of a civil war. Rumors of an Orlesian conspiracy to further destabilize Ferelden have led a mercenary to the town of Hafter’s Rest to investigate. This mercenary and their allies must work quickly to foil the plans of these spies, or else there may not be anything left of Ferelden in the wake of the Blight.
