#Important Notes
Nishie often wears a braid covering her forehead, using the hair from her bangs. She has particularly soft skin and wide hips.

#Story

While Nishie never knew her birth parents, she also has no interest in finding out if they are alive or who they were. She met Kyoko while she was still very young, and gladly became Kyoko's adopted daughter as soon as Kyoko was old enough.

Nishie figured out fairly early in her life that she was attracted to women, and Kyoko's support allowed Nishie to accept this as early as middle school. As such, she doesn't feel much in the way of angst for being homosexual, though she does dislike how her country is unwilling to acknowledge that part of her.
