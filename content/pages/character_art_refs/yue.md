#Important Notes
Yue prefers solitude or the company of very close friends, and thus can come across as standoffish. As a teenager, this gave her a temperamental and bitter personality when others didn’t respect her personal space. While she slowly learned to mask her discomfort and open up to people, she continues to prefer keeping to herself.

Having been raised by overly affectionate parents, Yue finds it difficult to express positive emotions without feeling embarrassed or uncomfortable. Because of this, she sometimes doesn’t admit when she starts to open up to people, even to herself.

Despite how difficult she finds it to relate to adults or people her own age, Yue turns out to have a talent for interacting with children, to the surprise of most who know her. She has far more patience for young children being unruly than she has for misbehaving adults, and is able to conceal the nastier side of her personality when dealing with children.
