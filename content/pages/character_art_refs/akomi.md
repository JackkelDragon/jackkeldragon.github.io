#Important Notes
Akomi has a larger and heavier build than most of her friends. Aside from her general physique, she also has a hyperactive metabolism that makes her always feel hungry, causing her to overeat. (However, even if she was not overweight she would still be taller and thicker-built than others.)

Akomi is very casual and playful, even around new acquaintances, and her willingness to make perverted jokes often rubs people the wrong way. However, there are things she takes seriously, and she is unwilling to intentionally cause anyone distress. In particular, she is very conscious of romantic relationships, and considers communication and consent essential.
