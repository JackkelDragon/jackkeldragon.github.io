#Important Notes
Himeka usually wears her hair in a high side ponytail on her left side, with her bangs loose and often with some stray hairs around the hair ties. When she does let her hair down, it goes just past her shoulders.

Being much taller than most of her friends, Himeka usually wears shorts or spats when she wears a skirt.

Himeka is generally cheerful and outgoing. She’s also sensitive to the mood of a situation, often empathizing with how those around her are feeling. However, she can become stubborn when annoyed.

#Story
Himeka Uchida is the oldest of three sisters. She is friendly and cheerful, but lacks ambition. She drifted through school with average grades and skipped university to work at a convenience store in the city where her friends had moved to. She can become very loyal and invested in her friends, but this can come off as being a busybody trying to fix things for everyone.

Note: The details of Himeka’s life vary across the different world states (storylines) of the games she appears in. Some common traits are:

- Becoming friends with Izumi, Kiyomi, Nanami, and a few other girls during high school at Solana Academy.
- Moving to Akiramine to live close to her friends, many of them from her school years.
- Having conflict with her younger sister Ichika over her decision to skip university.

#Likes
- Spending time with her friends
- Finding ways to keep everyone happy
- Being introduced to new hobbies by her friends

#Dislikes
- Pointing out her height (reminds her of being teased for it)
- Heavy exercise
- Seeing her friends suffer
