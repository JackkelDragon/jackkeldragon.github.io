#Important Notes
Asa shares her sisters' slightly thicker frame, most obvious with her waist and thighs. She's also the shortest and bustiest of her sisters. Despite her apparently unathletic appearance, Asa has trained in Aikido for self-defence.

In public, Asa acts cute and bubbly or polite and refined depending on the needs of the situation. To those who aren't familiar with her inner thoughts, she can seem mercurial or two-faced if she shifts modes during a conversation. In truth, these are merely masks she uses as the need arises, and her true feelings more easily show when she likes or dislikes someone enough.

With those she lets her guard down with, Asa isn't afraid of being seen as "ugly" or a slob. She turns into a gremlin in private, and is willing to be blunt with her feelings to close friends (or despised enemies).
