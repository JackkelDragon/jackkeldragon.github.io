#Important Notes
Nanami’s hair color is usually depicted with a slight gradient, making it lighter near the top of her head and darker near the tips of her hair.

Nanami tends to adopt a shy or timid posture, especially as a teenager, as she lacks confidence in herself and her abilities. However, as she matures she becomes more confident while retaining her humility, and she eventually takes on a more relaxed and casual demeanor.

When angry or upset, Nanami can sometimes explode with frustration. After she grows in confidence she also becomes more assertive in certain situations, but only when she feels it to be necessary. (In particular, she will take the lead in intimate situations unless her partner wants to.)

#Story
Nanami Kobayashi is an only child, and considers herself to be spoiled by that upbringing. While she seems timid and lacking in confidence, she actually is very skilled in certain areas and is very caring when it comes to her friends. She skipped university to work at a bakery, where her manager has served as a role model for her own growth in confidence.

Note: The details of Nanami’s life varies across the different world states (storylines) of the games she appears in. Some common traits are:

- Befriending Himeka and her friends during high school at Solana Academy.
Moving to Akiramine to work at the bakery of Orino Murakami.
- A strong desire for exploring physical intimacy with a partner, having fantasized about it for a long time.
- A steady growth in confidence as she matures, while still remaining humble.
