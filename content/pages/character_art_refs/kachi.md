#Important Notes
Kachi has poor eyesight, and so generally always has her glasses on or nearby. She wears her hair in a poofy/fluffy style similar to a long bob.

Kachi often seems shy or polite in public, rarely opening up to anyone she's not familiar with. She can become very impassioned when talking about something she likes, or if someone angers her. She has had limited close friends in her life, so she can become very intense in her affection.

#Story

Kachi was born to parents who had expensive interests, and quickly became a burden on her disinterested parents. When she suffered from bullying during middle school, her parents chose to make her live alone for high school. This created a feeling of resentment in Kachi, which culminated in her being willing to disown her parents. After high school, Kachi allowed Kyoko Hayashi to adopt her into the Hayashi family, even though this technically made Kachi 'sister' to her lover, Nishie.
