#Important Notes
As a teenager, Misaki often styles her hair into curls/ringlets. As she grows into adulthood, she does this less often, leaving her hair down most of the time.

Misaki is friendly and personable during her childhood and teenage years, often trying to keep everyone happy. However, a series of events during high school caused a shift in her personality and outlook. While she is still polite to strangers after this change, she is much more wary of opening up to others. Over time, Misaki becomes more insular and invests most of her time in a select group of friends that she trusts.

While Misaki would resist admitting it (even to herself), she shares a number of personality traits with her mother, particularly stubbornness in the face of opposing viewpoints.
