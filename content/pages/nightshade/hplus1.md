A slice-of-life, coming-of-age novella with science fiction elements. Hatsu Kamiya was disabled during an accident at a young age, losing most of the use of her limbs. Given the chance to test prototype cybernetic replacements for free, she jumps at the chance to return to being “normal”. Unfortunately for her, fitting in as she was may no longer be possible..
