Kigenishi Mods in Age of Empires II: Definitive Edition

This page serves as a collection of information for the Age of Empires III: Definitive Edition mods that add Kigenishi-themed content to the game. Most of the content currently being considered for these mods focus on the island of Kigenishi during the Liberation Era.

Campaign: Princess Osan

Details

A three-scenario campaign focused on the rise of Princess Osan and the nation of Kanekita. The Majin are represented by a modified version of the Japanese.

Scenario: Founding of Guwon

Details

A scenario focused on the arrival of the Nam-eun refugee fleet in Guwon and the war with the Majin. The Nam-eun and Majin are represented by modified versions of the Koreans and Japanese, respectively.
