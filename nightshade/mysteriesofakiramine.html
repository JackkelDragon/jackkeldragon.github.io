Mysteries of Akiramine

TBA

Shizuka Hirose is no stranger to the supernatural. Her life upended twice by circumstances that defied human logic, she has decided to become a bulwark protecting ordinary people from the dangers unseen in everyday life. However, the world stubbornly follows the laws of reality when Shizuka investigates its mysteries with her friend and partner Yuri Tachibana, forcing them to struggle to get by between cold cases while the world mocks them.

Despite the setbacks they have faced, their persistence will soon be rewarded with fresh encounters with the paranormal. For better or worse, they have awakened to knowledge beyond that of most humans, and they can no longer lower the veil and pretend not to see the true nature of the world. And in turn, they can no longer hide from the secrets they sought to uncover.

Mysteries of Akiramine is a series of short stories featuring Shizuka Hirose and Yuri Tachibana.
