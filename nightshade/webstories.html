Rising Storm

Contains spoilers for Flowering Nightshade: Storm on Your Peace.

Several years after the formation of Storm on Your Peace, the band is approached by a fan that has followed them for a long time. This fan suggests that the band would do well to have a percussionist, and offers herself for the role. While the fan is skilled, Yae knows that musical talent is not the only factor at play in keeping her band together. She and her friends must determine if this prospective member is a good fit, or if she would only cause the same sort of drama that Yae has experienced before.
