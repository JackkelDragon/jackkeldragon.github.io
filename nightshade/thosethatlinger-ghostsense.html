A paranormal tragedy short story. Ishiko Yanagi has always been fascinated with death and the supernatural, and finds a kindred spirit in Asumi Niwa. Hoping to make paranormal studies a more acceptable field in the eyes of the public, they set out to make it possible for anyone to observe ghosts. But with their zeal for knowledge and recognition, they sometimes forget the very real danger in tampering with phenomena outside of human understanding...
